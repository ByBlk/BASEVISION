Thank you for your purchase.

Singleplayer

How to install:

1. Use Jennie's custom anims mod add-on folder.
https://www.gta5-mods.com/misc/custom-animations-add-on-customanims

2. Drag the .ycd files to clip_amb@.rpf file.

3. Put this line to menyoostuff/favoriteanims.xml

	<Anim dict="anim@aim_car" name="aim_car_clip" />
	<Anim dict="anim@stack_pistol" name="stack_pistol_clip" />
	<Anim dict="anim@aim_up" name="aim_up_clip" />
	<Anim dict="anim@male@aim_weapon_6" name="aim_weapon_6_clip" />
	<Anim dict="anim@male@aim_weapon_6_kneel" name="aim_weapon_6_kneel_clip" />
	<Anim dict="anim@male@aim_weapon_6_walk" name="aim_weapon_6_walk_clip" />
	<Anim dict="anim@male@aim_weapon_6_ak" name="aim_weapon_6_ak_clip" />
	<Anim dict="anim@male@aim_weapon_6_ak_kneel" name="aim_weapon_6_ak_kneel_clip" />
